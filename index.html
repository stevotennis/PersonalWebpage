My Story

I am a UCSD graduate currently working as a Systems Engineer at Cubic Corporation. I did some web development back in college and enjoyed it immensely. But my background in college was Computer Engineering with more focus on hardware and embedded programming. And with those background, I got my current position as a embedded programmer. While I do enjoy busting my head about embedded programming problems, I want to pursue web development because I believe it is a good way to create solutions that anyone with internet access can use.


So, here I am, taking CareFoundry course and learning skills to be an awesome web developer instead. It is my dream to work in a career where my contribution goes right back to the community.
